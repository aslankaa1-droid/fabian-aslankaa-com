---

## 1. CONCEPT

FABIAN follows a **bottling franchise** model, historically refined in the non-alcoholic beverage industry (Coca-Cola Bottlers, Pepsi Bottling Group): the parent company owns the trademark, technology, recipe, marketing, corporate brand and premium design; the local partner — an active water bottling plant — provides production, logistics and sales in the assigned territory.

The network launches **from Y3**, after a successful completion of the direct pilot in Moscow and Saint Petersburg.

---

## 2. TARGET FRANCHISEE

Candidates are active regional mineral / table water plants meeting the following criteria:

- own or contracted well with confirmed compositional stability over 24+ months;
- a glass bottling line with capacity of 5M bottles per year or more, or a plan to install one within 6 months;
- relevant licences and certifications (CU TR 021/2011, EAEU 044/2017, ISO 22000);
- own or partner regional HoReCa distribution;
- financial standing: RUB 200M+ annual revenue or equivalent committed financing;
- reputational standing: no major Rospotrebnadzor sanctions or negative cases over the past 5 years.

---

## 3. FRANCHISE PACKAGE

What the franchisee receives upon signing:

- **Right to use the FABIAN trademark** on the assigned territory (exclusively or multi-exclusively).
- **Technical specification and recipe** — full package of TS, regulations, incoming-control protocols.
- **Packaging design pack** — bottle, label, cap, pallet palette, merchandising.
- **Marketing support** — annual federal media plan, central PR, sommelier programme, access to global key accounts (hotel chains).
- **Training** — a 4-week onboarding course for production, QC and sales personnel.
- **IT infrastructure** — access to corporate quality-control systems, B2B portal, CRM.
- **Procurement pool** — unified terms on glass, label, caps.
- **Brand legal protection** in the territory — provided by the parent JV.

## 4. FINANCIAL TERMS

### 4.1. Initial

- **Entry fee** (lump-sum): **$200,000 – $500,000** depending on territory size and exclusivity.
- **Agreement term**: 7 years with auto-renewal subject to KPI delivery.
- **Minimum bottling volume**: **5M bottles per year** (3M in Year 1); a shortfall triggers a territory review.

### 4.2. Ongoing

- **Royalty**: **6–10%** of franchisee net revenue, payable monthly.
- **Marketing fee**: **2%** of revenue into the central brand fund.
- **Minimum annual purchases** of key branded components (label, swing cap) from suppliers certified by the parent company.

### 4.3. Deposit and Penalties

- Security deposit: **$100,000** — returnable upon successful completion of the agreement.
- Penalties for QC and brand-book breaches — from 10% to 100% of monthly revenue.

## 6. QUALITY CONTROL

Quality control is a mandatory network-wide condition that determines the right to use the brand.

- **Continuous laboratory monitoring** of every batch across 24 parameters (microbiology, ionic composition, organoleptics). Protocols are uploaded to the centralised QC system of the JV.
- **Scheduled site audits** — once every six months, conducted by the JV QC team.
- **Unscheduled audits** — triggered by any HoReCa partner quality claim.
- **Arbitration samples** — stored for 18 months at the franchisee and at the central laboratory.
- **Mystery shopping** in the HoReCa channel — quarterly.

**Penalties for breach:**

| Breach | Sanction |
|---|---|
| Brand-book non-compliance | Notice + 5–10% monthly revenue fine |
| Single-batch QC deviation | Batch recall at franchisee cost + 25% fine |
| Systemic QC breach (3+ in 6 months) | Termination, deposit forfeited |
| Unauthorised export outside territory | Termination + $500k penalty |
| Use of brand after termination | Lawsuit for 100% of historic revenue |
