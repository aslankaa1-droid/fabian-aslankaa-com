# FABIAN

## *Premium table water. Glass. HoReCa CIS.*

---

**Vision.** To become the No. 1 premium still water in the HoReCa channel of the CIS by 2031 — through glass, design and a sommelier programme with a local story and a European code.

**Market.** The premium glass water HoReCa segment in the CIS is worth **RUB 14–18 billion** per year; the niche is distorted by the withdrawal of imported brands and stands unattended.

**Product.** Still table water in premium glass, two formats — **0.33L** (room service) and **0.75L** (restaurant table), mineralisation 150–350 mg/L, shelf price RUB 220–520.

---

### Strategy — three phases

| Phase | Period | What we do |
|---|---|---|
| **1. Direct pilot** | Y1–Y2 | Moscow + Saint Petersburg, contract bottling, $4–5M round, 800 HoReCa points by end of Y2 |
| **2. Bottling franchise** | Y3 | Strategic fork based on pilot results; launch of 18–25 franchisees across Russian federal districts and CIS |
| **3. Global** | Y4–Y5 | Entry into the Middle East, Southeast Asia and island resorts via the JV in DMCC |
